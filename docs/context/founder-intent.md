# Founder Intent

## What This Site Must Feel Like

A secret, elite, next-generation materials lab. Not a startup homepage. Not a portfolio. Not a tech company landing page. A living research interface where the visitor encounters a breakthrough material sample from the future.

## First Impression Target

> "This is a next-generation materials lab."

## Emotional Stack (in order)

1. **Curiosity** — what is this material? What does it do?
2. **Credibility** — this feels real, serious, and funded
3. **Desire to be involved** — I want access before everyone else
4. **Mystery** — there is more here than I can see

## After Visiting, People Should Feel

- Curious
- Convinced there is something real here
- Eager to know more
- Like they want access before everyone else

## What This Is NOT

- A generic startup site
- A SaaS company
- A portfolio site
- A personal brand site
- A vague art project with no business gravity
- A cyberpunk gimmick
- A sterile academic institution
- A military dashboard
- A corporate sitemap
- A dribbble concept

## What This IS

- Secret lab interface
- Sexy, classy, dark, rich
- Artful and premium
- Highly interactive — closer to an installation than a website
- Mysterious but legible
- Elegant, not sterile
- Impersonal at first — artifact first, lab second, people later
- Deep enough to feel like a world

## Working Style Directive

- Be opinionated. Make choices.
- Favor depth over clutter
- Favor taste over novelty for novelty's sake
- Favor world-building and coherence over generic conversion patterns
- Tie every interaction to the same central thesis
- Do not brainstorm endlessly. Decide and build.
