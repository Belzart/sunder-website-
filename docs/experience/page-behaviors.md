# Page Behaviors

## Core Principle

Every page is a different mode of the same instrument. Not a different website. The blob persists. The field system persists. The cursor persists. Only the content and the "mode" of observation changes.

## Home / Material (/)

### Purpose
First encounter with the specimen. Encounter → curiosity → comprehension → desire.

### Emotional Role
Wonder, seduction, intrigue.

### Material Behavior
- Full-size blob, center of viewport
- All interaction modes active (grab, stretch, split, merge)
- Material color cycling active
- Maximum interactivity

### Content Visible
- Tagline: "The future is material."
- Thesis statement
- Domain cards (4 application areas)
- Floating properties (discoverable)
- Access CTA
- Lab annotations

### Content Withheld
- Detailed research areas
- Team information
- Specific capabilities
- Technical details

### Scroll Behavior
- Hero → Thesis → Domains → Properties → Access → Footer
- Each section entrance is scroll-triggered (whileInView)
- Generous spacing between sections

## Research (Future: /research)

### Purpose
Deeper view into what Sunder is exploring. Still abstract, but with more substance.

### Emotional Role
Credibility, technical depth, fascination.

### Material Behavior
- Blob shifts to a more analytical state (slower cycling, more subtle)
- Or: blob breaks into smaller specimens, one per research area
- Field influence on research cards/modules

### Content Visible
- 3–4 research area overviews
- Each with a name, a one-line description, and an associated material behavior
- Manifesto fragments interspersed

### Content Withheld
- Specific results, papers, data
- Team members working on each area

## Applications (Future: /applications)

### Purpose
Show downstream potential without overclaiming.

### Emotional Role
Grounding, practical imagination, "this could be real."

### Material Behavior
- Material shifts to industry-specific presets when hovering an application
- E.g., hover "Aerospace" → frost/metallic preset, hover "Biomed" → organic/translucent

### Content Visible
- 4–6 application domains with brief descriptions
- Each application changes the material state
- "Projected material behaviors" as lab-style data

### Content Withheld
- Specific products or prototypes
- Customer names or partnerships

## Access (/access)

### Purpose
The gate. Convert curiosity into contact.

### Emotional Role
Scarcity, exclusivity, commitment.

### Material Behavior
- Blob at reduced size or more ambient
- Field glow intensifies around the CTA
- Minimal distraction from the action

### Content Visible
- "Request early access to the research interface."
- Email: hello@sunder.com
- Partnership / Investment / Collaboration labels

### Content Withheld
- Everything else. This page is pure intention.

## Page Transitions (Future)

Navigating between pages should feel like the material reforming, not a page swap:
- Content fades out
- Blob morphs (scale, position, distort shift)
- New content fades in
- Total duration: 0.6–1.0s
- The blob is the constant — the content changes around it
