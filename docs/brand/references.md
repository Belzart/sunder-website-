# References

## Tonal / Atmospheric References

These are not visual templates to copy. They are examples of the feeling, atmosphere, or quality standard we are targeting in different dimensions.

### Lab Interface Feel
- Scientific instrument UIs (oscilloscopes, electron microscopes, mass spectrometers)
- CERN control room displays
- JPL mission control interfaces
- Medical imaging workstations
- The precision, not the clutter

### Premium Dark Aesthetic
- Bang & Olufsen product pages — restraint, materials-first, cinematic spacing
- Porsche Design — dark, minimal, confidence without shouting
- Aesop — restrained luxury, intentional typography, the product speaks
- Dieter Rams — function as form, nothing extra

### Interactive / Experiential
- Active Theory studio work — physics-driven interaction, nothing arbitrary
- Resn — tactile digital experiences, playful but not childish
- North Kingdom — cinematic web experiences
- The difference: our interactions serve a thesis (material science), not just spectacle

### Material / Scientific
- MIT Self-Assembly Lab — responsive materials, real research
- Morphing Matter Lab (CMU) — shape-changing interfaces
- Materiom — open-source materials, earnest but less polished
- These are peers in concept but we want 10× the brand quality

### Typography / Copy
- Kinfolk — restrained editorial, generous whitespace
- Monocle — confident, declarative, no filler
- Apple product pages (pre-2020) — one idea per screen, decisive language

## What We Are NOT Referencing

- Cyberpunk / neon / Blade Runner aesthetics
- Gaming UI / HUD elements
- Dribbble-style gradient-heavy dark themes
- Generic "futuristic" UI kits
- Web3 / crypto aesthetics
- Tech startup templates (YC demo day style)
- Notion / Linear / modern SaaS (too utilitarian)

## Competitive Landscape (Websites)

No direct competitor has a site like this. The closest comparisons:

- **Boston Dynamics** — good product content, generic site
- **Anduril** — better brand energy, still conventional layout
- **Figure AI** — strong video, weak interaction
- **Formlabs** — functional, not experiential

Our differentiation: the site itself is the experience. No other materials company has this level of interactive quality. The website IS the proof of concept.
