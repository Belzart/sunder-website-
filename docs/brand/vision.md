# Brand Vision

## The World of Sunder

Sunder exists at the frontier where material science becomes something closer to living systems. The website is not a brochure about this world — it IS the world. Visiting the site should feel like entering a research facility where a new class of matter is being developed.

## The Core Metaphor

The website is a lab interface. The visitor is an observer, then a participant. The material is the specimen. Every interaction is an experiment. Every page is a different mode of the same instrument.

## Emotional Architecture

### Layer 1: Encounter (0–5 seconds)
Dark. Quiet. A shape in the center. It moves on its own. Something is alive here.

### Layer 2: Curiosity (5–30 seconds)
The visitor moves their cursor. The material responds. It stretches, resists, deforms. "What is this?" becomes "What can I do with this?"

### Layer 3: Comprehension (30 seconds – 2 minutes)
Text appears. Restrained. "The future is material." The visitor scrolls. Thesis. Domains. Properties. The picture forms: this is a materials company, but not like any they've seen.

### Layer 4: Desire (2+ minutes)
The visitor wants in. They've felt something unusual. The experience itself is the proof of concept. "If this is what their website does, imagine what their material does."

## What the Website IS

- A lab interface for a future material
- An interactive proof-of-concept for the company's taste and ambition
- A filter: the right people will feel compelled, others will move on
- A world that rewards exploration

## What the Website Is NOT

- An explainer
- A pitch deck in HTML
- A portfolio of capabilities
- A marketing funnel
