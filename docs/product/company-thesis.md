# Company Thesis

## What Sunder Is

A stealth materials company exploring a new class of responsive matter. Currently conceptual and brand-driven. Eventually physical products and real frontier materials work.

## Positioning

- Stealth company now
- Could become many things later
- Brand-driven rather than over-explaining hard science at this stage
- Should feel like one core material now, with hints of a broader platform later
- Advanced materials lab exploring matter that could eventually matter across many industries

## What Sunder Does (Stated Simply)

Engineers matter that responds — materials that sense, adapt, and reconfigure under force, signal, heat, or field.

## Category

Next-generation materials. Not AI. Not SaaS. Not biotech exactly. A new category: programmable / responsive / active matter.

## Downstream Application Areas (Hinted, Not Claimed)

- Biomed
- Aerospace
- Robotics
- AI-linked physical systems
- Defense

These are hinted at as future possibilities. The site should NOT claim products in these areas. It should suggest that the underlying material platform could eventually serve them.

## What to Reveal vs. Hide

### Reveal
- That a new class of material exists
- That it is responsive, programmable, shape-shifting
- That it has broad potential applications
- That serious people are behind it
- That access is limited

### Hide
- Exact science or IP
- Team details (for now)
- Specific product roadmaps
- Funding details
- Technical implementation specifics

## Competitive Frame

There is no direct competitor to name. The frame is: "materials have been static for too long. We are making them active." The competition is the status quo of passive materials.

## Tagline

"The future is material."
